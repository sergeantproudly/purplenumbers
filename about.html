About Us

Purple Numbers is a leading telecommunications service provider, specialising in International Premium Rate Numbers, Premium Inbound SMS, DIDs, Value Added Services, and customised numbering solutions for businesses. We bring a completely new outlook to a traditional telecoms business, where we focus on innovations. We are new, but we have a significant experience in telecommunications, which helps us to pursue speedy growth and serve more than 1,200 satisfied customers across the globe.

We are a multicultural company working in six different countries with one goal: to serve the clients in the best possible way.

Our Vision

Purple Numbers is a customer-oriented company and our focus is to see the customer always happy with our services.

We want to expand our business and to reach a maximum number of aspiring people around the globe. Our priority is to have an outstanding reputation and dignity. Our ambition is to become a leader in the IPRN and in Other Value Added Services by applying best efforts and following ethical values of our clients and partners.

Our Mission

Our goal is to maintain relationship with clients and shareholders. We want our successes to speak for themselves. Our pillars are ethics, values, innovations, trust and credibility in the industry.

We protect the reputation of our company in every possible way. The dedication of our team and the efforts made by each individual are the key to our success and the faith of stakeholders, clients and partners. This faith keeps us motivated to achieve our goals.

Our team has a mission not merely to survive, but to thrive and to do so with some passion. We want to reach a point where clients will immediately think of Purple Numbers when in need of a service such as ours.
